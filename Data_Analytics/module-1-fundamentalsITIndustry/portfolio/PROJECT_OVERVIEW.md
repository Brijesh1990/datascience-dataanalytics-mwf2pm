## 🎨 Customization Guide

### Change Your Name & Title
1. Open `index.html` in text editor
2. Find your name in the hero section
3. Replace with your own
4. Save and refresh browser

### Add Your Photo
1. Prepare a 400x400px professional photo
2. Save it in `images/` folder as `profile.jpg`
3. Refresh browser

### Update Skills & Experience
1. Edit relevant sections in `index.html`
2. Update percentages and skill names
3. Add your work experience
4. Add your education

### Change Colors
1. Open `config.css`
2. Modify color values at the top
3. All colors update automatically

See [README.md](README.md) for detailed customization steps.
